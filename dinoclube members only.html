Dinofamily tree

dinoclube members only.

the two main sections of the dinosaur family tree are: saurischia and ornithischia.we will start with the saurischia.Saurischia has two (3) main grops as well.the first one is theropods and the second one is sauropodomorpha (don't forget eoraptor).we are looking into the theropods.

theropods split into 4 groups: coelophysoidea,dilophosaurus,neoceratosauria,tetanurae.we are looking into the tetanurae.The tetanurae splits into 3 groups: spinosauroidea,allosauroidea,coelurosauria.We are doing the spinosauroidea.

Now it's up to you to find out about the spinosauroidea.
